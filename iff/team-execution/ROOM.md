This founding team has an unusual combination of advantages for this specific domain. The gaps are equally important to name honestly.

What you have:
- Operational credibility: combat command in Gaza, Lebanon, Syria -- you are the user
- Technical depth: sensor fusion + computer vision for autonomous systems (teammate)
- Legal coverage: land registration / property law background covers IP and structuring
- Recon vehicle experience: operational context for platform-based IFF variants

What may be missing (to assess):
- Defense procurement expertise: who has navigated Israeli or NATO procurement before?
- Business development: who has sold to military buyers?
- Regulatory/ITAR: export control for dual-use defense technology

Starter questions:
1. What does your teammate bring beyond sensor fusion -- does he have defense industry contacts, security clearances, or experience with military certification processes?
2. What is the one capability gap that, if unfilled, most threatens your ability to execute in the next 18 months?
3. Are you both full-time on this, or is this currently a side project? That answer determines your realistic timeline.
