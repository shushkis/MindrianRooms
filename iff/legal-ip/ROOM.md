Defense tech has one of the most complex legal landscapes of any startup domain. ITAR (International Traffic in Arms Regulations), Israeli defense export law, patent strategy for dual-use technology, and company structure for government contracting are all live issues from day one -- not when you scale. Your legal background is a genuine moat here.

Starter questions:
1. Is the core IP in your solution patentable, or is it better protected as trade secret / classified capability? The answer changes your IP strategy entirely.
2. Israeli defense technology export requires DDEC (Defense Export Controls Department) licensing. Have you begun mapping what categories your solution falls into?
3. If you intend to sell to US or NATO forces, what company structure do you need -- and does a foreign-founded company face barriers to SBIR/STTR or OTA vehicles?
