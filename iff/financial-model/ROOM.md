Defense tech financial models look nothing like commercial tech. Revenue is lumpy, cycles are 18-36 months, and the path to revenue typically runs through grants and R&D contracts before any product revenue lands. Model for survival, not growth, in years 1-3.

Starter questions:
1. What is your runway assumption to first contract? Most defense tech startups need 24-36 months of capital before seeing product revenue. What is your funding plan to bridge that gap?
2. Are there Israeli defense innovation grants (MAFAT, BIRD Foundation, Israel Innovation Authority) or dual-use R&D programs your team qualifies for that could extend runway?
3. What is the unit economics of your solution at scale -- per-soldier cost, per-unit cost, and what margin structure a defense prime would demand if they become your channel?
