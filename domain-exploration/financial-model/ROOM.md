Financial modeling is premature until the domain is selected and a customer archetype is defined.

Starter questions:
1. What does the first contract look like -- size, duration, deliverables?
2. What does the cost structure look like in year one (compute, data, people)?
3. Is there a grant or non-dilutive funding path relevant to this domain (Israel Innovation Authority, NIH, EU Horizon)?
