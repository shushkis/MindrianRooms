Business model exploration is early-stage. Key questions to answer after domain selection.

Starter questions:
1. What does the buyer's current budget look like -- and what line item would this replace?
2. Is this a SaaS tool, a services engagement, or a data product?
3. What is the unit of value delivery -- per case, per patient, per jurisdiction?
