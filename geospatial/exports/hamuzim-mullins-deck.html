Well-Defined Problem

The Evidence Gap

Big Market

How can land registration authorities reach a legally defensible verdict when historical evidence is fragmented across disconnected archives unlinked to geographic coordinates -- resolving disputed parcels that today default to the state by procedural failure rather than by fact?

MVP

How can we help the state identify parcels where private ownership cannot be proven, given that 70% of West Bank land is unregistered and historical records are disconnected from geographic coordinates?

Strongest Domain

Domain 1 -- Irreversible Pain

When a parcel defaults to the state, the private claimant loses everything permanently. A system that changes that outcome -- even probabilistically -- has a near-unlimited willingness-to-pay ceiling relative to its cost. No other domain in this analysis has this property.

Domain 6 -- Ottoman OCR/NLP

Initially flagged YELLOW. Upgraded to GREEN after establishing: an active Ottoman-Turkish OCR research community exists (Osmanlica.com, 88-97% character accuracy), and LLM-based error correction on Ottoman text reaches a 1.16% character error rate. Live Update -- Diligence Never Stops

Domain 3 -- Re-Checked

Two real competitors surfaced after this analysis was first written -- a reminder that a Mullins score card is a snapshot, not a verdict.

Orhitec GIS (Israel, est. 1999)

Real-time land registry / permits / tax GIS for local authorities. No historical imagery, no Ottoman records, no unregistered-land focus. Not a capability threat -- a distribution-speed watch item (27 years of government trust).

GEOward (Israel, 22 years)

Manual-expert aerial photo interpretation as legal evidence -- serves private real-estate lawyers and municipalities on already-registered land. Genuinely direct on capability; different customer (private disputes, not the Custodian / unregistered land / Ottoman documents).

Net: "zero direct rivals" narrows to "zero rivals in the specific niche," not "zero competitors in the category." Domain 3 stands at GREEN with a flagged narrowing -- worth a live re-score before the next commitment gate.
