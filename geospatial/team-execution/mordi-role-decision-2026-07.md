# Founder Decision -- Mordi's Role

## The Decision

Mordi is a **co-founder**, not an advisor or external design partner. He
will use his existing government/institutional relationships to kick off
the MVP conversation, and has committed to going full-time if the venture
needs it.

## Role Split

- **Mordi:** product and business direction, using his domain expertise
  and relationships as the primary channel to the MVP buyer (the
  government property custodian's office).
- **Elli:** technical build and product architecture.

Fundraising and hiring decision rights not yet explicitly divided --
carried forward as an open item.

## The Firm Relationship -- Resolved

Mordi's law firm is **not** a paying customer today, and there is no
commercial relationship between the firm and HaMuzim. The firm's role is
**freemium / proof-of-value**: a capped number of free queries, offered so
the firm can road-test the product and generate a citable proof-of-value
case -- separate and distinct from the actual commercial go-to-market
target (the government custodian's office / ISA). This resolves the
founder-self-dealing pricing question raised in
`mordi-role-prep-2026-07.md` -- there's no price to set because there's no
sale, by design, at this stage.
