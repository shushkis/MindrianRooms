## Why This Is Serious, Not Just Awkward

Per Israel's Civil Service Regulations (תקשי"ר):

- A civil servant **may not engage in any private work outside their role
  without a permit ("היתר")** -- including unpaid involvement, if it
  creates a conflict between the role and the private activity.
- When there's doubt about whether a conflict exists, **the Civil Service
  Commission (נציבות שירות המדינה) decides**, not the employee.
- The standard mechanism is a **"הסדר למניעת ניגוד עניינים"
  (conflict-of-interest prevention arrangement)**, prepared by the legal
  advisor of the specific government ministry involved.
- This is a live, working process, not a theoretical hurdle -- as of end
  of 2025, **6,007 state employees hold a valid permit for private
  work**. It's done regularly. It is not done informally.
- Per a separate source, breaching conflict-of-interest prohibitions for
  public officials is described as a **criminal offense**, not just a
  disciplinary matter.

Sources: [Civil Service Commission conflict-of-interest info sheet](https://meyda.education.gov.il/files/edu-hub/hr/NIGUD.pdf), [Dafna Shmuelevitz -- private work and permits](https://dsh-law.com/circular/%D7%9C%D7%A2%D7%95%D7%91%D7%93-%D7%9E%D7%93%D7%99%D7%A0%D7%94-%D7%90%D7%99%D7%9F-%D7%96%D7%9B%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%A7%D7%A0%D7%95%D7%99%D7%94-%D7%9C%D7%A7%D7%91%D7%9C%D7%AA-%D7%90%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%95%D7%A8/), [State Comptroller -- rules and arrangements for preventing conflict of interest](https://library.mevaker.gov.il/sites/DigitalLibrary/Pages/Reports/1846-2.aspx), [UNODC -- Israel Ministry of Justice submission](https://www.unodc.org/documents/treaties/UNCAC/WorkingGroups/workinggroup4/2018-September-6-7/Contributions_NV/Israel_EN.pdf)

## What This Does Not Mean

It does not mean the plan is dead. 6,007 civil servants currently hold
legitimate private-work permits -- this is a known, navigable process,
not an automatic disqualification. But it does mean the sequence of
events matters: **the permit and conflict-of-interest arrangement need to
exist before HaMuzim does business with Mordi's own office**, not after.
Any revenue, contract, or even informal pilot with that office ahead of
a proper arrangement is real legal exposure for Mordi personally, not
just a company-level risk.
